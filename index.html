Why $SANTA?

Santa Doge is an ERC-20 token on the Ethereum blockchain that is designed to help fund the development and marketing of the project. The token has a unique tax system where 4% of every buy and sell transaction is distributed to the development and marketing team to support the growth of the project. The name of the token is a play on the popular "Doge" meme and the term "degen," which is short for "degenerate" and refers to someone who takes high risks for potential rewards. The project hopes to use the funds generated from the tax to continue improving and promoting the token, making it a valuable asset for investors and users alike.
